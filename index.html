MY FAVORITE PINOY FOOD

CHICKEN ADOBO

Ingredients 30 g canola oil 24 g garlic crushed 30 g onion, sliced 1 kilogram chicken cut ups 30 g vinegar 60 g soy sauce 240 g water 4 g bay leaves 2 g whole black peppercorns, slightly crushed 2 pc Knorr chicken cubes 4 g brown sugar packed Option: 30 g kale or spinach

PORK ADOBO

Ingredients 20 grams vegetable oil 15 g garlic, crushed 35 grams onions, minced 500 g pork belly, sliced 10 g (1 pc) Knorr Pork Cubes 45 ml Knorr Liquid Seasoning 100 ml water 25 ml vinegar 3 pcs laurel leaves 8 g brown sugar 2 grams whole black pepper

BULALO

Ingredients 750 g bulalo water to cover 70 g onions, quartered 50 g garlic, mashed 15 g black peppercorns 1 L broth 2 pcs. Knorr Beef Cubes siling labuyo or hot sauce 350 g potatoes, quartered 500 g noodles, cooked 200 g pechay, trimmed
